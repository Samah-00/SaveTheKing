Meet the Enemy.
Moving ghosts will block the way of the four characters.
Each character has three lives, crashing into a ghost will
decrease a life from the character.
However, a character can win a life back by collecting
a healing kit.
A "kill all the animes" present might appear in some levels,
any character can collect the gift by walking on it.
Finally, collecting a speed up present will increases the speed
of the enemies.
